    PROFESSIONAL DOG GROOMING

    Mutt Cuts grooming van is equipped with state of the art equiptment and experienced and gentle groomers. Whether your pup visits for a complete groom with haircut, or just a simple, sudsy bath, we'll provide him or her with all the kindness and comfort possible. We use only the best, all natural shampoos and conditioners. Each pup visiting our spa is also given walks by the van and offered water.
